Meet Joe & Our Approach

The Joe Shanks Story

Joe's journey began right here in North Dorset. His passion for movement led him to study Exercise Science, followed by a degree in Physiotherapy. This blend of academic knowledge and local roots forms the foundation of his practice.

Joe takes a truly holistic approach, viewing the body as an interconnected system. This philosophy, backed by a deep, diverse toolbox of techniques, means that whether you are dealing with a fresh acute injury or struggling with persistent chronic pain, effective help is available.
